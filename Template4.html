About Our Cafe

The ambiance of the cafe, its homey aroma, draws my soul into its cocoon for a few blessed moments.Cafe tables adorn the sun-kissed grey as if placed there at the tip of an artists brush.The coffee cup was a thought-sanctuary, allowing my dreams to find warm depths amid the aromatic foam.

In the coffee shop we are as awkward lovers, pretending to be there of convenience, afraid of our need to connect to one another. This place filled with people always so close and so far apart... this is the community of our era... as close as we can be without admitting the truth.THANK YOU.
